about



I'm Mouad and I'm a French Engineer from Paris.


My strong background in math coupled with my passion for data/quantitative analysis have naturally pushed me to thrive in the data science field. I currently work as sr. data scientist at Tesla, where I enjoy enabling data-driven decicion making with very various datasets.


My interests are various but mainly focused around two areas :

They have in common the need for a deep understanding of the data but also for the ability to take a step back and understand the "real" world.
