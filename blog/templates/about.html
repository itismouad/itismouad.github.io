Areas of Expertise


In simple words, I enjoy using my technical skills to bridge the gap between complex problems and business value creation. This passion for enabling decision-making naturally pushed me to thrive in the data science field.


My areas of expertise are mainly focused around two areas :

  • Data: Data/Decision Science, A.I. deployment, Data Organization
  • Economics/Finance: Business Strategy, Investment Research, Financial Modeling

They both have the need for a deep understanding of the data but also for the ability to take a step back and be curious about the real world.


Example of the projects I have been working on :

  • Management of data analysts and scientists (recruitment process, onboarding, mentoring, etc.)
  • Automation of data pipelines and quantitative analyses including executive-level metrics, P&L statements, etc.
  • Deployment of A.I. algorithms to automate text data processing and categorization in collaboration with US regulator NHTSA
  • Development of warranty forecasts for Tesla Battery Pack and Drive Unit, handling communication with external financial auditors
  • etc.
